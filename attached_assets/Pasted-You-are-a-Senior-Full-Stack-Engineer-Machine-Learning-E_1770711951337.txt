────────────────────────────────────
USER ROLES (VERY IMPORTANT)
────────────────────────────────────

1. STUDENT
- Can SIGN UP and LOGIN
- Can see ONLY his/her own data
- Can:
  - Enter academic details
  - Answer stress questions
  - View:
    - Risk score
    - Risk level
    - Progress over time
    - Recommendations
  - Play stress-relief games
- Cannot see other students

2. TEACHER
- Can SIGN UP and LOGIN
- Can:
  - Add multiple students (basic profile)
  - View list of students under their account
  - Enter / update academic data for students
  - View:
    - Individual student progress
    - Risk trends
    - Stress indicators
- Teacher CANNOT see students from another teacher

────────────────────────────────────
TEACHER → STUDENT FLOW
────────────────────────────────────

1. Teacher signs up
2. Teacher adds a student (name + email)
3. System asks student to SIGN UP using that email
4. Student logs in and:
   - Sees assigned teacher
   - Can view own dashboard
   - Can play stress-relief games
5. Teacher can later:
   - View student’s progress
   - Monitor risk levels
   - Take academic assessment decisions
